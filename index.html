About Us

Leora

Leora is a trusted tailoring establishment located in the heart of Perungudi, Chennai, serving the local community with dedication and craft. From stitching new garments to rescuing your favourite outfits, Leora brings skill and attention to detail to every piece of clothing that comes through the door.

With loyal customers spanning over many years, Leora specialises in custom blouse stitching, jeans alterations, and design-based stitching from photos or references. Conveniently located on Panchayat Main Rd in Phase-1, Elim Nagar, the shop is open six days a week to serve your tailoring needs.

  • Open 6 days a week from 9 AM to 9 PM for your convenience
  • Design replication from photos for personalised garments
  • Trusted by loyal customers in Perungudi for years
